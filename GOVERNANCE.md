# Governance

## Decision-Making

Seabay is currently maintained under a **BDFL (Benevolent Dictator for Life)** model. All project decisions — including roadmap, architecture, releases, and community policy — are made by the project maintainer.

**Maintainer:** Avan Mo (avan@seapex.ai)

## Contributions

Contributions are welcome via pull requests. The maintainer reviews and merges all PRs. There is no formal committee or voting process at this stage.

## Evolution

As the project and community grow, this governance model may evolve. Any changes to governance will be documented here and communicated in advance.

## Contact

For governance questions, email avan@seapex.ai.
